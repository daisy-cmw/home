The Gap Method

When do we use this?

In Permutation & Combination, if a question states that certain items "must be separated" or "cannot be on consecutive days", we use the Gap Method.

  1. Step 1: Arrange the unrestricted items first.
  2. Step 2: Identify the gaps (spaces) between and around these items.
  3. Step 3: Select and arrange the restricted items into those gaps.

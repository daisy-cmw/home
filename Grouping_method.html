The Grouping Method (Block Method)

When do we use this?

In HKDSE Permutation questions, if specific items "must be together" or "must be next to each other", we use the Grouping (or Block) Method.

Step 1 (Internal): Treat the restricted items as a single "block". Find the number of ways to arrange them inside that block.

Step 2 (External): Treat that entire block as ONE single item. Combine it with the remaining items, and find the total number of ways to arrange all units externally.

Problem Scenario: Ann wants to publish 5 videos (3 Travel videos and 2 Food videos) in a row over 5 days. However, the 3 Travel videos must be published on consecutive days (they must stay together). In how many different ways can she arrange her schedule?
